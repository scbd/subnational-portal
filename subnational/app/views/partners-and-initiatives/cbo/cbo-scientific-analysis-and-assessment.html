CBO - Scientific Analysis and Assessment

The Global Urbanization, Biodiversity, and Ecosystems – Challenges and Opportunities is a more detailed scientific analysis and assessment of the links between urbanization, biodiversity and ecosystem services that is scheduled to be published as book in 2013. It provides extended, in-depth coverage of regional and local case study assessments.

The draft core chapters of this volume are now available online.
